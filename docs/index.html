Massage Therapy Clinic

Las Vegas, Nevada, USA

200+ Inquiries at ~$5 Each

The Problem

The clinic needed a consistent flow of inquiries to fill appointment slots and reduce empty schedules.

What I Did

● Launched targeted Facebook Messenger ad campaigns
● Focused on local wellness audiences
● Optimized creatives for direct messaging conversions

The Result

● 224 inquiries generated
● $1,022 ad spend
● ~$5 cost per inquiry

Key Insight

While the campaign generated strong inquiry volume, it exposed a common issue:
Not all inquiries were being converted into bookings.
This highlighted the need for better follow-up and response systems which is where most clinics lose potential clients.
